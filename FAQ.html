Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. What are your opening hours?
      2. We’re open every day from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Drop by anytime for your favorite coffee or a quick bite!
  2. Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?
      3. Yes, we do! We offer a variety of plant-based milk options, including almond, oat, and soy milk, to customize your coffee. We also have vegan pastries and treats available.
  3. Where do you source your coffee beans from?
      4. We carefully select our coffee beans from sustainable farms in regions like Ethiopia, Colombia, and Costa Rica. Our beans are roasted to perfection to bring out the best flavors in every cup.
  4. Do you offer free Wi-Fi?
      5. Absolutely! Our coffee shop provides free Wi-Fi to all customers. Feel free to bring your laptop and enjoy a productive work session or study group with a delicious coffee by your side.
  5. Can I order online or place a pickup order?
      6. Yes, you can! We offer online ordering through our website and mobile app for both pickup and delivery. Just choose your items, place your order, and we'll have it ready for you.
  6. Do you cater for events or offer large orders?
      7. Yes, we do cater for events! Whether it’s a business meeting, birthday party, or any special occasion, we can provide coffee and pastries for your guests. Contact us for more details and pricing.
  7. Are pets allowed in the coffee shop?
      8. We love furry friends! Pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area. However, for health and safety reasons, they are not allowed inside the main shop area.
  8. Do you have a loyalty program?
      9. Yes! We offer a loyalty program where you can earn points with every purchase. Collect points to enjoy discounts, free drinks, and special rewards. Ask our barista for more details when you visit!
  9. Do you have gluten-free options?
      10. We do offer a selection of gluten-free pastries and treats. Let our staff know about any dietary restrictions, and we’ll be happy to help you choose the best options.
  10. How can I contact you for any other inquiries?
      11. You can reach us at interstel4rmurph@gmail.com for any questions, feedback, or special requests. We’re always here to help!
